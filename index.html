About the Program

Bring brand-new productions to life

These are fully realized student short films. In past years, films from this program have been shown in film festivals across the Bay.

We're casting actors of all ages (7–70) across a wide range of genres, with opportunities for lead and supporting roles.

Beyond the screen, working on these productions is a great way to connect with the Bay Area film community.

The Process

What to expect

01

Sign Up & Submit

Fill out our sign-up form with your contact info, resume, and a headshot. Select a 15-minute audition slot that works for you between 9am–4pm — slots are limited, so sign up early.
02

Your Audition

Come prepared with a short (1–2 min) monologue. We might also ask you to do a cold reading but no prep needed for that. Expect a relaxed, collaborative atmosphere — our filmmakers want you to succeed.

03

Callbacks & Casting

Actors will be contacted within a day for callbacks or a direct offer. Principal actors will need to be available for a 2–3 day shoot sometime between March 30 and April 12.
