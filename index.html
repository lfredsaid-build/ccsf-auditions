About the Program

Bring brand-new productions to life

Student filmmakers at CCSF are producing fully realized short films and are casting actors in principal and featured roles.

Past actors have gone on to screen at local film festivals and collaborate on ongoing creative projects with filmmakers across the Bay Area.

Whether you've never been on camera before or you've got a full resume, this is a great opportunity to partner with folks in the Bay Area film community.

The Process

What to expect

01

Sign Up & Submit

Fill out our sign-up form with your contact info, experience level, and a headshot. Select an audition slot that works for you — slots are limited, so sign up early.

02

Your Audition

Come prepared with a short (1–2 min) monologue or cold-read from sides we'll email you beforehand. Expect a relaxed, collaborative atmosphere — our filmmakers want you to succeed.

03

Callbacks & Casting

Actors will be contacted within a day for callbacks or a direct offer. Cast actors will need to be available for a 2–3 day shoot sometime between March 30 and April 12.
